CAMP & HOTEL IN SCHOVICE

The Camp Lávka Park is located in a picturesque valley near the dam lake in Vrané nad Vltavou, 5 km south of Prague. The size of the whole area is 25,000 m²

Accommodation. A hotel-type accommodation facility is available with the capacity of 22 double rooms.

Camp. Besides the outstandingly beautiful setting the camp is sought after for its proximity to the centre of the city.
